What is Bioacculumation?


What is Bioaccumulation? Bioaccumulation is the process by which harmful substances, such as pollutants or toxins, accumulate in the tissues of living organisms over time

This phenomenon occurs when the rate of intake of a substance is greater than the organism's ability to excrete or metabolize it.

This can have serious consequences for both individual organisms and entire ecosystems, as it can lead to the magnification of harmful substances up the food chain.
